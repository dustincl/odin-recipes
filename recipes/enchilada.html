Enchilada Casserole

Description

Definitely delicious, if not authentic.

    Ingredients
  • Enchilada sauce packet
  • Tomato paste
  • Water
  • Ground beef
  • Garlic Salt
  • Onion Powder
  • Refried Beans
  • Corn Tortillas
  • Shredded cheese
    Instructions
  1. Get the shit. Preheat 375.
  2. Mix the sauce packet with water.
  3. Cook the beef with garlic and onion, drain.
  4. Mix refried beans and sauce with beef.
  5. Dip tortillas in sauce and line casserole dish.
  6. Spoon half the meet and half the cheese on top.
  7. Cover with another layer of dipped tortillas, beef mixure, and cheese.
  8. Cover with more saucy tortillas and cheese.
  9. Bake covered for 20 minutes.
