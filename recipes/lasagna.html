Lasagna

Description

This is some tasty, easy lasagna. Garfield would be happy.

    Ingredients
  • Beef
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodle
  • Water
    Instructions
  1. Cook beef and stif in sauce
  2. Combine eggs, cheeses, and seasonings
  3. Assemble lasagne
  4. Bake covered for 45 minutes
  5. Uncover and keep baking for 10 minutes
