Oven-Roasted Asparagus

Description

Amazing. Well worth the funny smelling pee.

    Ingredients
  • A bunch of trimmed asparagus
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Minced garlic clove
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lemon juice
    Instructions
  1. Preheat 425
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and toss
  3. Put in baking dish, sprinkle with oil, cheese, salt and pepper
  4. Bake until tender (10-15 minutes)
  5. Squeeze out some lemon juice
